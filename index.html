About Us

Diverse products, one brand, endless possibilities.

Canada Garlic Importing Inc. is a dynamic company specializing in the wholesale distribution of a diverse range of high-quality products. From fresh garlic and onions to aluminum foil, frozen chicken, fish, and a variety of plastic cups and bags, we cater to the needs of businesses seeking top-notch supplies. Our commitment to excellence ensures that our clients receive premium products at competitive prices, making Canada Garlic Importing Inc. the go-to choice for a one-stop solution in wholesale purchasing.
